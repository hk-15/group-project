This week’s hot spots

Ramble Cafe, E7

Nestled under a railway arch a short walk from the buzz of Forest Gate, Ramble Cafe is easy to miss, but the combination of delicious baked goods, friendly welcome and community vibe make this a must-visit.

Cakes on offer range from the classics to more adventurous flavours, and the toasties never disappoint. There’s outdoor seating for sunny days, but beware this is a popular local spot so tables fill up fast. Get there early in the morning or a bit after lunch to ensure you get a spot.

Recommended by Holly, chips&coffee co-founder

Tofu Vegan, N1

There are three Tofu Vegan restaurants in London – in Islington, Spitalfields and Golders Green – but no matter which you go to, expect to find fast service, expertly cooked tofu and top-tier dim sum. Those expecting sleek interiors and an instagrammable moment may be disappointed, but any misgivings will soon be swept away by the arrival of the food.

The totally plant-based menu will delight vegans and vegetarians and might just win some fans among others. No trip to Tofu Vegan is complete without a dish of the wontons in the house special sauce.

Recommended by Anna, from the chips&coffee community
